Educational Video Recommender - Adaptive Information Systems- INFSCI 2480

  • Built a recommendation system that has a collection of a educational youtube videos on various technical topics and subjects taught at Information School.
  • Developed user based algorithms to perform collaborative filtering, content based recommendation and personalization. Further implemented social navigation to allow users to discover interests from other similar users ranked by their social reputation. (Php, MySql, Ajax, Html)
Strengths
  • Social navigation - following the footsteps of the other users by visiting that user's profile and clicking on the recommended links.
  • Social recommendation based on the tag score and category score of reputed users.
  • Adaptive presentation - organizing links in user profile and in home page.
  • Visibility of all ratings and categories of every other user in the system.
  • Flexibility to change the recommendations based on by updating profile anytime .
  • Mostly collaborative - but it suffers from cold-start, so we have use content-based in the beginning.
  • Explicit feedback is more useful than implicit - that is why ratings have more weightage than views - also the user profile show rated videos instead of viewed.
  • Binary ratings for simplicity - negative values included for better recommendation - Important in videos because users invest more time.
  • Feature based ratings for more meaningful ratings - rather than a simple like or dislike about a video - offers more clarity to other users.
  • Team Members: Hao Wu, Shruthi
